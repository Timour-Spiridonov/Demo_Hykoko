ABOUT HYKOKO MOUVEMENT

HYKAKO ODIO® is a serie of cultual events, based on various music genres, mainly electronic and alternative music. Our ambiton is to create a unique experience by combining Theatre, Dance, Music, Artistic Performance, Fashion Promenade ...

Made as a performance of living arts and entertainement, the focus of these events is highlighted through images, aesthetics and atmospheres.

The visitor becomes an actor in the performance, no longer a consumer but rather a participant.

TOLERANCE

PARTICIPATION

COLLABORATION

Our agency encourages the creative cooperations and collaborations between Tunisian artists and young talents from various movements and artistic fields such as:
- Sound arts: celebration of all music genres.
- Performing arts: theater, dance, mime, circus .
- Visual arts: visual arts, cinema, photography.
- Language arts: novels, fables, stories, poetry, etc.
- Spatial arts: architecture, space design...
We strive to produce, promote and enhance artworks and communication methods that favour such interaction.
